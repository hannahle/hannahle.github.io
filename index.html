I'm passionate about accelerating research in sciences and improving human's health.

I grew up in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. At 16, I moved to Canada by myself and later joined the Li Ka Shing Institute to research how to engineer an artificial ear cartilage for burned victims.

I joined The Knowledge Society, where I worked on various projects, including reversing aging in Drosophila, building genetic algorithms, and developing an online adaptive framework for radiation therapy at Princess Margaret Hospital.

Now I'm working on a collaboration tool for researchers that enable you to quickly search for experiments and protocols that others have done. Think of it like Github x arXiv - but for sciences. If you find this interesting, feel free to reach out!
